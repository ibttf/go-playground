## Overview

<Accordion title="Is there a basic introduction to Go?" active>
  Yes, we have an entire section dedicated to the basics of Go. It covers the
  syntax, how to write functions, and the fundamentals of concurrency. You can
  find all of this in the ["Basics"](../basics/why-go) section.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you offer guides or tutorials on more advanced Go topics?">
  Absolutely! We have a section titled
  ["Guides/Tutorials"](../guides/guides-and-tutorials) that offers insights into
  more advanced topics such as building a backend, crafting microservices,
  creating CLI applications, and connecting to databases using Go.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How frequently is the content updated?">
  We aim to keep the content up-to-date with the latest in the Go community and
  the evolving best practices. We periodically review and update our guides and
  tutorials.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I request a specific tutorial or guide?">
  While we don't have a direct feature for that currently, we always appreciate
  feedback and suggestions. Feel free to reach out with your ideas, and we'll do
  our best to incorporate them.
</Accordion>

## Support

<Accordion
  title="How can I provide feedback or report an issue with the content?"
  active
>
  We value feedback from our community. If you've found an error or have
  suggestions, please reach out to our support email or use the feedback form on
  the site.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you offer any resources or references for further reading?">
  Yes, at the end of many of our guides and tutorials, we provide additional
  resources, references, and further reading materials to help you deepen your
  understanding of Go.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a community or forum for discussing Go topics and questions?">
  While our site is primarily focused on tutorials and guides, we highly
  recommend the Go community forum and the Go subreddit for community
  discussions and further questions.
</Accordion>

## Security & Privacy

<Accordion title="How do you handle user data?" active>
  We respect the privacy of our users. While we do collect basic analytical data
  for improving user experience, we do not sell or share this data with third
  parties.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is my browsing history on the site kept private?">
  Yes, your browsing history remains private. We utilize basic cookies for site
  functionality, but we do not track or store detailed user browsing patterns.
</Accordion>
